My Major

I am a cyber security major.

Program Overview:
  • The program prepares students for careers as cybersecurity professionals.
  • It focuses on essential concepts of cyber-defense and the analytical fundamentals of cybersecurity.
  • Students learn about risk management, cyber-defense techniques, and strategies for ensuring the safety of online information.
  • The curriculum covers topics like cybersecurity analytics, visualization, and risk analysis.
  • Graduates are equipped to join the rapidly growing cybersecurity workforce across various organizations.
What Is Cybersecurity Analytics and Operations?:
  • Cybersecurity deals with protecting computer systems, networks, programs, and data from attacks and unauthorized access.
  • This program goes beyond traditional IT fields and focuses on:
  • Analyzing cybersecurity data: Identifying cyber incidents and understanding malware actions.
  • Communicating concerns: Effectively conveying critical information to business stakeholders and the public.
  • High-performing cyber analysts possess a strong background in mathematics and computational skills.
Career Paths:
Graduates can pursue various roles in the cybersecurity field:
  • Security Analyst: Monitoring and analyzing security incidents, implementing security measures, and responding to threats.
  • Incident Responder: Investigating and mitigating security incidents, ensuring rapid response to breaches.
  • Security Consultant: Advising organizations on security best practices, risk assessment, and compliance.
  • Penetration Tester (Ethical Hacker): Identifying vulnerabilities by simulating attacks on systems.
  • Security Architect: Designing and implementing secure systems and networks.
  • Risk Analyst: Assessing and managing cybersecurity risks.
  • Cybersecurity Manager/Director: Overseeing security operations and strategy.
Campus Options:
  • You can study this program at any Penn State campus or through Penn State World Campus (online).
